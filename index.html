About Mark Kickhofel:

With nearly 25 years in support of government contracts as a Systems Integration Specialist, Mark Kickhofel has used his technical knowledge to begin a successful transition into the field of Lighting Design. For the past 4 years, Mark has worked with Baltimore, MD band, Swamp Donkey New Grass to lead and support the integration of a variety of different types of lighting equipment into their performances. Working with the band members and venues, he uses his open-minded approach to lighting design, allowing him the ability to prevent and overcome challenges that may arise. From design conception through execution, Mark creates a visually immersive experience for the audience leaving them wanting more.
